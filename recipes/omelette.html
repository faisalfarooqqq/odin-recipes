Omelette

Description

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cut the onions into small pieces.
  2. Heat the frying pan ad put in oil.
  3. break the eggs in a bowl, add salt & pepper, cheese, and onions.
  4. Mix them and pour it into the frying pan
  5. flip it so both sides are evenly cooked
